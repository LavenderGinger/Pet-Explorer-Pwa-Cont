Guinea Pig

Guinea pigs are gentle, vocal animals that thrive in pairs or groups. They love fresh veggies, hay, and squeaky social interaction.

  • Lifespan: 5–7 years
  • Diet: Herbivore
  • Temperament: Sociable, gentle, vocal
