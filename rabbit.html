Rabbit

Rabbits are gentle and social creatures that love to hop, burrow, and chew. They thrive with space to move, lots of chew toys, and kind company.

  • Lifespan: 8–12 years
  • Diet: Herbivore
  • Temperament: Gentle, social, playful
